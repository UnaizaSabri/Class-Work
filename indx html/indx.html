MacBook Air

Lightweight and portable, ideal for everyday tasks and casual use. Known for its long battery

MacBook Pro:
More powerful, designed for professionals like designers, video editors, and software developers. It features higher RAM and better processors.

Operating System: macOS:
Each MacBook runs the latest version of macOS, receiving regular updates for security, performance, and new features.

Connectivity: Ports:
Recent models come with Thunderbolt 3/USB-C ports for data transfer and charging.

Price Range:
Prices vary based on the model and specifications, generally placing MacBooks in the premium price segment.

Uses:
Suitable for daily tasks, office work, creative projects, programming, and entertainment.
